GINHAWISE, the upgraded pang-agiw, is better than traditional ceiling cleaners because it eliminates the need for ladders—reducing fall risks for seniors, PWDs, and solo homeowners. Its ergonomic, energy-efficient, and low-noise design improves comfort, air quality, and sustainability. By allowing users to clean from a distance, it protects health by minimizing exposure to molds and allergens, making Filipino homes safer and more inclusive. Equipped with a rotating and tilting head, interchangeable attachments, and an extendable ergonomic rod, GINHAWISE adapts to various ceiling surfaces. Its UV light module reveals hidden dirt and mold, while the mist-to-foam spray disinfects without direct contact. A live-streaming camera with Bluetooth connectivity offers real-time guidance, and hazard detection sensors alert users to safety risks. Powered by both solar and battery, it’s a smart, sustainable tool rooted in Filipino ingenuity.
